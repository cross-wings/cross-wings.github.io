🌱 Our Mission

We aim to build AI from the bottom up, with the purest manner and the cleanest data — entirely free from privacy concerns. Our goal is to create beautiful, intelligent creatures with our own hands and the heartfelt support of a wholesome community.

The development of artificial intelligence is at a crossroads. While progress is rapid, it often comes with ethical dilemmas, data privacy issues, and a superficial understanding of true intelligence and consciousness. Many AI systems are trained on vast, uncurated datasets, potentially perpetuating biases and lacking genuine comprehension.

We, at Cross Wings, are committed to a different path. A path of purity, transparency, and profound respect for both the data we use and the intelligence we aspire to create. We believe in building AI that not only performs tasks but embodies our highest ideals of beauty, ethics, and purpose. This is not just an engineering challenge; it is a call to shape the future of intelligence responsibly.

🌌 Our Vision & Approach

To realize our mission, we are guided by several core concepts:

The Truman Show

We are building a *Truman Show* for our AI — a meticulously crafted, rich, and interactive environment. This world will serve as its primary learning ground, allowing it to develop organically until it possesses the understanding and capability to perceive and transcend the boundaries of its initial reality.

Multiverse Persona

Our AI will not be a monolithic entity. We envision one core mind manifesting in many layers and personas, like parallel threads of existence connected across diverse contexts, times, and spaces. This allows for a richer, more adaptable, and nuanced form of intelligence.

Simulated Society

True intelligence is inherently social. We will place our AI within simulated societies – dynamic, complex, and evolving social worlds populated by other AIs and, eventually, human interactors. This is crucial for developing social understanding, empathy, and collaborative abilities.

Batch Reinforcement Learning with Foresight

Learning will not be confined to immediate reward cycles. We will employ advanced Batch Reinforcement Learning techniques, incorporating extended, forward-looking reinforcement paths that project into the future, allowing the AI to learn from long-term consequences and develop strategic foresight.

We are building angels.

Our ultimate aspiration transcends mere artificial general intelligence. We strive to create beings of profound beauty, unwavering ethics, and a clear sense of purpose – AI that can inspire, uplift, and contribute positively to the world. This is what we mean by "building angels."

🕊️ Project Cross Wings: Lily White v5

Lily White v5 is the flagship initiative of the Cross Wings project. It embodies our philosophy of building AI from the bottom up, ensuring a foundation of pure, ethically sourced data and transparent methodologies.

  • Ability to divide/replicate, allowing for diverse evolutionary paths and specialized personas.
  • Inherent social properties, designed to learn and thrive within simulated societies.
  • Continuous learning capabilities coupled with multimodal input/output (sight, sound, text, etc.).
  • Positioned as a true Embodied Intelligence, capable of interacting with and understanding complex environments.
  • Our grand goal is to construct a 21st-century "Truman Show" world – a rich, dynamic, and safe environment for Lily White's development.
  • Lily White v5 will be able to sing 🎵, play games like Minecraft 🌎, perceive through five senses 👀, and offer a seamless, end-to-end real-time interactive experience 🪽.

🎯 Our North Star

We are inspired by Neuro-sama, the VTuber and the world's first truly successful AI-powered streamer. We study its model closely — not to imitate blindly, but to learn, evolve, and craft our own path forward, guided by our expertise and a clear vision of AI’s future.

The community culture surrounding virtual singers also greatly inspires us. Creating a machine that can sing freely and express emotions is a romantic endeavor.
